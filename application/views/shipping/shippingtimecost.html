PICK UP IN STORE
You can order products on our shop.diesel.com and collect them from a Diesel Store of your choice. All you have to do is to select "Ship to a store near you" as your preferred delivery option at checkout and choose which store you'd like to have your order delivered to. The service is offered at no additional cost and the order will be delivered within 7-10 business days. You will receive an email when your order is ready for pick up. Remember to bring with you the order ID (printed or on cell phone).

US TAX POLICIES
Tax is based on the state and zip code in which the order is shipped to and all taxes will be added to the order total at checkout.
